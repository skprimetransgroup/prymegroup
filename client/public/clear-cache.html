🔄 Cache Clearing Tool

If you're seeing a blank page or outdated content, this tool will clear your browser's cache for Pryme Group.

Manual Steps (if needed):

  1. Safari (iPhone): Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data
  2. Chrome (Mobile): Menu → Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data
  3. After clearing: Force close your browser app and reopen it
  4. Then visit: primetransgroup.ca
